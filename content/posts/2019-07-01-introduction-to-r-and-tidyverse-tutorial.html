Learn the art of creating efficient, readable, and modular pipelines.

What is a Pipeline

You can wrangle data. You can move data. You can clean the data. How? You create data pipelines. So what exactly is a data pipeline?

The goal is to have an efficient flow of data from one location to the other in today’s data-driven enterprise. Analysis cannot begin until the data becomes available to someone who understands it. There are so many things that can go wrong during the transfer from one data system to another: data can change through manual intervention or data sources may conflict, data can be ill-defined.

The data pipeline is the automation of taking data from many sources (extracting) and transforming them into usable data. Then the data can be loaded into models that can perform visualizations or even make decisions.

What is the Tidyverse

The Tidyverse is a tool to do Data Science. It is a collection of packages that will help with creating data pipelines and make life much easier!
