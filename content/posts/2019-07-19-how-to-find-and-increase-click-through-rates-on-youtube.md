<span class="st">Impressions click-through rate (CTR) is a performance metric which is measured as the percentage of people who watched your video after seeing an impression. The rate should be between 3-6%. Here, I will show you how to find CTR and how to improve it<br /> </span>

## How to Find Click-Through Rates on YouTube:

Go to youtube.com and **login** to your account. You will find an icon on the top right, click on it. Now, click on **Youtube Studio(beta)**

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-631" src="http://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR.png" alt="" width="882" height="505" srcset="https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR.png 882w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR-300x172.png 300w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR-768x440.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 882px) 100vw, 882px" />

&nbsp;

Now, in the left menu, click on **Analytics**. Here, you will find different tabs like overview, reach viewers, interest viewers, build an audience and earn revenue.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-632" src="http://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR1.png" alt="" width="911" height="439" srcset="https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR1.png 911w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR1-300x145.png 300w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR1-768x370.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 911px) 100vw, 911px" />

&nbsp;

Click on **Reach Viewers** tabs and you will see impressions click-through rate.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-633" src="http://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR2.png" alt="" width="911" height="441" srcset="https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR2.png 911w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR2-300x145.png 300w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR2-768x372.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 911px) 100vw, 911px" />

&nbsp;

## How to Improve Click-Through Rates on YouTube:

Now, go back to the **Overview** tab. You will see top videos, scroll down and click on see more. You can sort these videos according to CTR by clicking on **Impressions click-through rate**.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-634" src="http://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR3.png" alt="" width="889" height="441" srcset="https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR3.png 889w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR3-300x149.png 300w, https://markgingrass.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/CTR3-768x381.png 768w" sizes="(max-width: 889px) 100vw, 889px" />

&nbsp;

The videos with higher CTR is at the top and with lower CTR at the bottom. Now, you check the thumbnails of videos with higher CTR and compare it with the videos with lower CTR. Now, the idea is that when you create better thumbnails, you should have higher CTR with time.

To change the thumbnails, go back to the **Overview** and click on videos from the left menu. Just, click on the video, there is an option of a custom thumbnail.
